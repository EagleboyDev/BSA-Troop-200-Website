Boy Scout Troop 200 Information On How To Join

All you have to do to join Boy Scout Troop 200 is come visit us in person, any monday night at 6:30, as long as the schedule says we will not be at an event. We are located at "Seelyville United Methodist Church" at "2351 2nd St, Seelyville, IN 47878". When you arrive just walk in the front doors and take a left down the stairs, and ask who to talk to for joining.
