Boy Scout Troop 200 Other Things About Us

We are Boy Scout Troop 200, an awesome Troop in Seelyville Indiana. We currently have 18 Scouts in our Troop. We take Scouting very seriosly but also have copius amounts of fun. We are located at "Seelyville United Methodist Church" at "2351 2nd St, Seelyville, IN 47878". When you arrive just walk in the front doors and take a left down the stairs. Come visit us any monday (Unless we are at an event posted on the Event Schedule), our Troop meetings run from 6:30 - 8:00. We hope to see you soon!
